Welcome to Idle Clicker Empire. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile game.

TL;DR: We don't collect personal information. Your game progress is stored locally on your device. We use AdMob for ads.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information We DO NOT Collect

We do not collect, store, or share:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Contacts or photos
  • Financial information
  • Any data that can personally identify you

1.2 Local Storage

Your game progress is saved locally on your device using:

  • localStorage: Stores your coins, upgrades, generators, and achievements
  • This data never leaves your device
  • You can delete this data at any time using the in-game reset button

1.3 Third-Party Services

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Ad interaction data
  • Anonymous advertising identifiers

AdMob's data collection is governed by Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2. How We Use Information

Since we don't collect personal information, there's nothing to use! The only data usage is:

  • Local game saves: To restore your progress when you reopen the app
  • AdMob: To show you relevant advertisements (managed by Google)

  • Your progress is only accessible on your device
  • If you uninstall the app, all data is deleted
